What can we drink?

Fresh fruits Juice
Freshly pressed juice from your own juicer, a juice bar or cold pressed juice. Make sure it is strained well to remove the fiber.
Fresh Veggies Juice
Freshly pressed juice from your own juicer, a juice bar or cold pressed juice. Make sure it is strained well to remove the fiber. TIP: Juicing leafy greens becomes much easier if you roll the leaves into a little ball, before feeding it into the machine.
Water
Alkaline water, artesinal water or Pure Spring water
Herbal Teas
fruity, minty, rooibos, bitters, health blends..., just make sure the ingredients don't have anything suspect, no caffeine
Miso
Try to find organic paste
Chlorophyll
Add it to your water
Seeds
Sesame, Flax, Chia, Pumpkin, Sunflower, Hemp
Spirulina
It is loaded with protein and all the vitamins and minerals the body needs. Mix it with your liquids.
Wheatgrass
or other grasses like barley grass
Kombucha
a fermented probiotic drink. Watch out for the sugar content. Some contain as much sugar as a soda.
Coconut Water
I prefer the white Young Thai coconuts, or the green Carribean Coconuts, however, bottled coconut is allowed. Harmless or other raw brands are preferred.

What should we avoid?

Food
Any foods, live/raw or cooked
Fiber
fiber will cause us to experience hunger. Strain the juices well
Store brought juices
Bottled, canned or carton juices (unless it is cold pressed)
Smoothies
Bananas
and other fruits that are not easy to separate fiber from the juice.
Drugs and Alcohol
Yep
Caffeine
Coffee, Caffeinated teas, Soda
