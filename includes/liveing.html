What can we eat?

Fresh fruits
Including but not limited to Apples, Pears, Citrus, Bananas, Berries, Coconuts, Tropical Fruit, Melons. Try to avoid seedless fruits.
Fresh Veggies
Green Leafy Vegetables, Root Vegetables, Peppers, Mushrooms
Sea veggies
Sea Moss, Nori, Dulse, and Arame. >> Learn more about sea vegetables
Fermented Food
Sauerkraut, Kimchee, Miso
Sprouts
Alfalfa Sprouts, Bean Sprouts, Sprouted grains
Whole Grains
Brown rice, Quinoa, Millet, Farro, Wild Rice, Amaranth >> Learn more about Whole Grains
Beans
Lentils, Black Beans, Chickpeas... any dried bean
Oils
Virgin Avocado, Extra Virgin Olive, Coconut
Seeds
Sesame, Flax, Chia, Pumpkin, Sunflower, Hemp
Salty Stuff
Sea Salt, Crystal Salt, Liquid Aminos, Tamari, Shoyu
Sweeteners
Honey, Maple Syrup, Agave Nectar, And Stevia
Dried fruits
Sun-dried Tomatoes, Figs, Dates, Raisins, Cranberries, Currants - Try to find varieties with no sugar added
Herbs/Spices
Thyme, Rosemary, Turmeric, Dried Spices, Fresh Herbs. If you are using a spice blend, read the ingredients to make sure there are no forbidden ingredients.

What should we avoid?

cooked Food
Any food heated above 115 degrees
Pasteurized Beverages
Packaged, boxed or processed juices
Plant-based Meat
Tofu, Tempeh, Seitan, TVP, Beyond Meat, Impossible Meat, And Other Mock Meats
Processed Food
Bread, Pasta, White Rice, White Sugar, Table Salt, Canned Foods, White sugar, Turbanado sugar, Raw sugar, Pancake syrup
Nuts
no nuts
Dairy
Milk, Cheese, Butter, Yogurt, Kefir
Fast Food
Artificial Ingredients
MSG (aka Monosodium Glutamate), Artificial Colors, Artificial Sweeteners (spenda, sweet-n-low, equal), Artificial Flavors
Low Quality Oils
Hyddrogenated Oils (Margarine, Crisco, partially hydrogenated oil), soybean oil, canola oil, vegetable oil
Drugs and Alcohol
Yep
Caffeine
Coffee, Caffeinated teas, Soda
