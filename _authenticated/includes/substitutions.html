Food Substitution Chart

One major step in becoming healthier is making better food choices. The foods below are healthier options that you can swap out in your favorite recipes. Reading labels is very important. The main ingredients that we should avoid are:

  • Hydrogenated oils
  • Partially hydrogenated oils
  • Monosodium Glutamate (aka MSG).
  • High fructose corn syrup
  • Artificial Sweeteners (including Aspertame and Sucrolose - aka Splenda)
  • Artificial Colors
Below is a more extensive list of ingredients to avoid, and healthier substitutions for each.
